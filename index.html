Bacardi
Gold

₱ 1,300

Bacardi Gold is a smooth, rich, and flavorful amber rum crafted through a unique blend of charred oak barrel aging and a special charcoal filtration process. With notes of buttery caramel, vanilla, toasted almonds, and hints of banana and orange peel, Bacardi Gold delivers a warm, mellow finish. Its balanced flavor makes it perfect for classic cocktails like the Cuba Libre and Rum Punch or enjoyed simply on the rocks. Bacardi Gold embodies the bold Caribbean spirit in every sip.

Absolut

Absolut Vodka is a premium Swedish vodka known for its smooth, clean taste and iconic clear bottle. Made from natural ingredients, including winter wheat and pure well water, Absolut is distilled multiple times for exceptional purity. Its subtle grainy flavor with hints of dried fruit makes it perfect for cocktails or enjoying straight.
